####  Installation:
    Put files dinput8.dll to your root game folder.
    win32 >> bin\win_x86
    win64 >> bin\win_x64

####  Using:
    Put your mods\plugins to Documents\Euro Truck Simulator 2\TFuncs
    Enjoy!
